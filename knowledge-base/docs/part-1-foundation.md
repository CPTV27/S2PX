# PART I: COMPANY FOUNDATION & TEAM

## Company Identity & Positioning

- **Legal Entity:** Scan2Plan, Inc., a Delaware C Corporation founded in 2018.
- **HQ:** Troy, NY with an additional hub in Brooklyn, NY.
- **Category:** As-Built Assurance.
- **Core Promise:** "Guaranteed Certainty, From Capture to Coordination."
- **Mission:** To deliver trust (transparency), confidence (proven standards), and integrity (accountability) on every project.
- **Vision:** Scan2Plan is the measure of excellence for Architects and Engineers.

## The Three Guarantees

1. **Transparency & Standards:** We define, designate, and deliver LoD & LoA on every project -- backed by our 12-Point Assurance Standard.
2. **Price-Match:** If a competitor can match our standards, we will match their price.
3. **Lifetime Support:** Assistance for the life of the project, including unlimited revisions within contracted scope.

## External Language Rules (CRITICAL)

Consistency in how we describe our teams builds trust and protects our vendor relationships.

- **Modeling Team:** Always refer to them as "our dedicated modeling team." Never use the vendor name externally.
- **QC Team:** Always refer to them as "our QC team" or "our dedicated quality control team." Never use the vendor name.
- **Prohibited Terms:** Do not use "in-house," "no outsourcing," or "no third-party vendors".
