### 4.A ‚Äî Funnel Stage Dictionary
Stages: Lead ‚Üí Engaged ‚Üí Meeting ‚Üí Opportunity ‚Üí Proposal ‚Üí Closed (Won/Lost)
Gatekeeper Rules:
- No Opportunity without a qualified Meeting logged and categorized
- No Proposal without a completed Scope Audit and estimated margin view
- No stage advancement without Source + Assist attribution populated
- No late-funnel follow-up without proof discipline satisfied ("1 link + 1 proof")
